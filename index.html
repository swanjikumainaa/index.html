MY FIRST WEEK AT AKIRACHIX

My first week at AkiraChix has been thrilling. I have had a few challenges here and there but I have enjoyed most of my experiences which have been breath taking.

Good things Experienced

  1. The food is scrumptious.
  2. Beautiful immaculate rooms.
  3. Hands on study method.
  4. Serene environment.
  5. Providence of quality and adequate personal effects

Not so good things experienced

  • Inadequate hanging lines
  • Lack of breaks to visit family and friends
  • Denial to use phones during the day
  • Strict dresscode
